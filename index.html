Know the Difference

Nano vs.
Microblading

Two techniques, two different results. Here's how to choose the right one for your skin type and lifestyle.

Microblading
Manual blade technique
  • Best for normal to dry skin
  • Hair-like strokes drawn manually
  • Retention: 1–2 years
  • Slightly more surface trauma
  • More natural, feathered look
  • Not ideal for oily or mature skin
vs
Nano Machine
Digital machine technique
  • Works on all skin types
  • Ultra-fine needle, precise hairstrokes
  • Retention: 1–4 years
  • Gentler on the skin
  • Sharper, more defined result
  • Our most recommended technique
