Nilash Studio

Our Story

Est. 2018 · Tampa, FL

Born from a belief that beauty should be effortless

Nilash Studio began in 2018 with a single conviction: permanent makeup, done right, should free you — not define you. Too often, we'd seen clients walking around with PMU that looked heavy, artificial, or simply wrong for their faces. We wanted to change that.

Our founder trained with master artists across Europe and North America, studying not just technique but the deeper philosophy of enhancing what's already there. The result was a studio built on restraint, precision, and a deep respect for each client's natural beauty.

Today, Nilash Studio is home to a small, dedicated team of PMU artists who share that original vision. We take fewer clients per day than most studios — because every face deserves unhurried, undivided attention.

"We don't believe in trends. We believe in results that will still look beautiful a decade from now."

— Nilash, Founder
