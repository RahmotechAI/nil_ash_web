Our Philosophy

Open Education.
Real Techniques.

We believe the PMU industry grows stronger when artists share knowledge generously, not guard it selfishly. At Nilash Academy, you'll learn what actually works — the methods, the troubleshooting, the mistakes — with complete transparency.

Whether you're brand new to permanent makeup or a working artist looking to refine your nano technique, our training programs are built around one thing: helping you produce results you're genuinely proud of.

"The best artists in this industry give back. We train the next generation to be better than us."

Your Instructor

Trained by the Best.
Teaching the Rest.

Nilash has been working in permanent makeup since 2016. She trained under master artists in Amsterdam and New York before opening her own studio in Tampa in 2018. Since then, she's become one of the most sought-after PMU educators in the Southeast.

She teaches the way she learned: by doing. Her training programs emphasize real technique, honest troubleshooting, and the kind of nuanced judgment that only comes from years of work on real clients with real faces.

When she's not at the studio or teaching, she's usually photographing her work, developing new curriculum, or convincing people that the best brows look like they've always been there.
