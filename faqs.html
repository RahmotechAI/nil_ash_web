Before Your Appointment

Permanent makeup (PMU) is a cosmetic tattooing technique that implants pigment into the upper layers of the skin to create the appearance of makeup. Unlike traditional tattoos, PMU pigments are designed to fade gradually over time — giving you natural-looking results that can be refreshed as needed.

Most permanent makeup sessions take 2.5–3 hours, including consultation, design, and the procedure itself. Eyeliner sessions are slightly shorter at approximately 2 hours. Always arrive with a few extra minutes to spare.

We use a topical numbing cream before and during every procedure. Most clients describe the sensation as mild pressure or a slight scratching feeling. Pain tolerance varies, but the overwhelming majority of our clients are comfortable throughout. If you're concerned, let us know and we'll take extra care.

To prepare for your appointment:

  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before your session
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications (aspirin, ibuprofen) for 48 hours if possible
  • Do not use retinol or exfoliants near the treatment area for 1 week prior
  • Come with a clean face — no makeup in the treatment area
  • Eat a light meal before your appointment
  • For lip blush: apply SPF lip balm daily for 2 weeks before your session

Most healthy adults are good candidates. However, PMU may not be suitable if you are pregnant or nursing, have certain skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis near the treatment area), are undergoing chemotherapy, or take blood thinners regularly. We review each client's health history during the consultation — please be upfront with us so we can advise you honestly.

Microblading uses a hand tool with tiny blades to create cuts in the skin, while nano machine hair strokes use a digital machine with a single ultra-fine needle. Nano is gentler on the skin, suitable for all skin types (including oily and mature), and typically retains pigment better over time. We recommend nano for the vast majority of our clients — view our full comparison on the homepage.

After Your Appointment

The first 2 weeks are the healing phase. You'll experience:

  • Days 1–3: The area appears darker and more defined than the final result
  • Days 4–7: Light scabbing or flaking occurs — do not pick
  • Days 7–14: Pigment looks lighter as skin regenerates (this is normal)
  • Week 4–6: True final colour settles and a touch-up can be assessed

Full healing takes 4–6 weeks. The touch-up appointment at 6–8 weeks perfects the final result.

We provide a full written aftercare guide at your appointment. The key rules:

  • Keep the area dry for 7–10 days (no swimming, saunas, intense sweating)
  • Apply the provided aftercare balm as directed
  • Do not pick, peel, or scratch the treated area
  • Avoid direct sun exposure — use SPF once healed
  • No makeup on the treated area until fully healed

A touch-up at 6–8 weeks is highly recommended for first-time clients. It allows us to assess retention, fill any areas that didn't hold pigment, and refine the shape. Touch-up pricing varies by service — ask during your consultation. Annual colour refreshes keep results looking their best long-term.

Retention varies by person, skin type, and lifestyle. Generally:

  • Nano brows / Hybrid brows: 1–4 years
  • Lip blush: 1–4 years
  • Permanent eyeliner: 2–5 years

Oily skin, sun exposure, and certain skincare products (retinol, AHAs) can shorten retention. Annual colour refreshes are recommended to maintain vibrancy.
