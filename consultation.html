Photo Guidance

Tips for Your Photos

✕

No Makeup

A clean face helps us see your natural features, skin tone, and any existing brow hair we'll be working with.

☀

Natural Light

Step near a window or go outside. Artificial lighting distorts skin tone and makes it harder to assess results.

◎

Straight-On Angle

Face the camera directly, chin level, eyes open. This gives us the clearest view of your facial symmetry.
